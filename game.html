The Game

Melovar's Tale is an inspired roguelike deckbuilder where every card drawn, every relic discovered, and every decision made carves a unique path through a world of fire and shadow in the land of Eodolas. I wanted this game to have hints of the overarching story, fun game mechanics and provide something for the playerbase to enjoy.

Core Mechanic

Unique Cards

Every card in Melovar's Tale has been crafted with deliberate mechanical design. From devastating fire strikes, impenetrable ice barriers, to multistrike combos, each card offers unique strategic possibilities.

Build your synergies across attack, skill and power archetypes. Discover powerful combos that chain across card types, relics and even utilise the cards from other heroes if the opportunity presents itself. The possibilities are as vast but sometimes come with a price. Carefully make your choice and ensure the darkness doesn't overwhelm you... Or even Greed!

Game Changer

Powerful Relics

Relics are the major differences that can define your run and playstyle. Each one bends the rules of the game and with hundreds of combinations, can lead to game breaking moments which are there for you to explore! Relics from powerful enemies, hidden within events and the legendary Boss relics can ultimately find you that rush of being able to take on the hardest of enemies and uncover secrets.

Discover these legendary relics which reshape entire strategies. Stack synergies, trigger chain reactions and watch your build come alive as you travel through the map.

Infinite Replayability

Roguelike Depth

No two runs of Melovar's Tale are ever the same. Navigate procedurally generated maps with branching paths, discover secret events that test your decision making and face escalating encounters that push your deck and strategy to its limits.

Choose between risk and reward at every crossroad. Will you take the shortcut to power through tribute or sacrifice, or play it safe through the path with least resistance? Every choice matters. Every path can suddenly change your fate and be ready to fail and try again!

Core Mechanics

Every system in Melovar's Tale is designed to create deep, rewarding gameplay.

Strategic Combat

Utilise an array of unique cards with status effects, keyword interactions, and tactical depth that rewards mastery and creative thinking to defeat your enemies.

Branching Paths

Procedurally generated maps with multiple routes. No run is ever the same. Choose battles, unique events, treasure rooms and discover hidden moments as you forge your path.

Multiple Heroes

Select a unique hero, each with distinct starting decks and special abilities. Unlock new cards and synergies that open up realms of creativity.

Meta Progression

Earn XP across runs to unlock permanent upgrades, new cards, perk trees, and Ascension ranks that increase the challenge exponentially. Find yourself on the Leaderboard and show the world your ability.

Boss Encounters

Face terrifying bosses with unique mechanics and multi phase battles. Some with a pattern that may be understood and some with chaotic movesets. Adapt your strategy on the fly or be consumed by the darkness.

Story Events

Discover narrative events with meaningful choices that grant powerful relics, transform your deck, or challenge you with secret encounters.
